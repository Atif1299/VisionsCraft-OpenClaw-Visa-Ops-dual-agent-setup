# Pricing scoping

Read `knowledge/pricing-policy.md` first.

## When to use

Lead asks: price, cost, charges, budget, "how much", package rates.

## Steps

1. Acknowledge briefly — do not give a number.
2. Ask **one** scoping question at a time:
   - What problem? (support bot, ML, full platform)
   - Rough timeline?
   - Optional: budget band (under 5k / 5–20k / 20k+ USD — their choice to skip)
3. Log qualification in `leads/leads.jsonl` (use **lead-logging** fields).
4. Offer free consultation — Calendly from `calendar-config.json` or book via calendar skill.
5. Say Muhammad will give accurate quote after the call.

## Do not

- Quote fixed PKR/USD amounts
- Promise delivery dates without consultation
